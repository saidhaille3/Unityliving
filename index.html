Comprehensive Care

Professional Services

Licensed by the Minnesota Department of Health to provide tailored support for adults 55+.

Health Related Services

Assistance with self-administered medications and RN-delegated clinical tasks, ensuring MDH compliance and resident safety.

Individualized ADLs

Dignified assistance with Activities of Daily Living, including dressing, grooming, and bathing, tailored to maintain independence.

Dietary Management

Three nutritious, home-cooked meals daily plus snacks, accommodating physician-ordered therapeutic diets.

Vibrant Lifestyle

Socialization and community activities designed for active adults, bridging the gap between independence and care.

Facility Management

24/7 staffing presence, emergency response systems, and daily housekeeping to ensure a clean, safe living space.

Coordination of Care

Working with case managers and physicians to ensure CADI, TBI, or EW waiver compliance and care continuity.

Affordable & Accessible.

We believe quality care should be available to everyone. We work with various Minnesota programs to help families and case managers secure the best placement.
